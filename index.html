About Me

I’m a frontend developer focused on building websites that are simple, fast, and actually useful for the people using them.

I didn’t start out trying to impress with complex designs. My focus has always been understanding what users need when they land on a website and making sure they can find it quickly, without confusion.

Most of my work is centered around business websites and landing pages. I pay attention to structure, responsiveness, and performance, because those are the things that directly affect how a website performs in the real world.

Right now, I’m continuously improving by building real projects, refininng my approach, and learning how to turn ideas into functional products that solve actual problems.
